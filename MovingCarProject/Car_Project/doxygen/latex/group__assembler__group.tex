\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This group provides a good handful of macros intended to smooth out the differences between various assemblers, similar to what \mbox{\hyperlink{compiler_8h}{compiler.\+h}} does for compilers, except that assemblers tend to be much less standardized than compilers.
